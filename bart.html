Bartosz Domanski Studio Photography - Lighting Test

Project Description

Taken in Semester 2 of Photography. The class was spent learning how to use different forms of flash in different positions in order to make each photo look different. I took a sinister approach and had Bart hold the flash towards his face, illuminating only his shirt and the bottom half of his face.

Camera Details

  • Nikon D3100
  • f/5.3
  • 1/100sec
  • ISO-100
