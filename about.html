The Person Behind the Portfolio

Bio

I'm Shannon Keogh, an aspiring concert photographer and (hopeful) graphic designer. I'm in my first year of Creative Digital Media in the Institute of Technology, Blanchardstown. I have basic knowledge of web development (HTML and CSS) and graphic design. By basic, I mean pretty basic as I am pretty new to the world of graphic design and website development. However, I have previously dabbled in photography and media. Throughout my course I hope to build up my experience and knowledge.

Key Skills

  • Extremely outgoing and capable of dealing with work colleagues and the public in a professional and pleasant way
  • I am very thorough and organized in all tasks
  • I am fun, friendly, motivated and easy to work with.

Personal Achievements

Over the years I have won medals for cricket and also many different types of dance (ballet, ballroom, tap, show to name a few)
In 2013 I took part in a competion as part of Squares Day in Dublin and came first place, earning me a prize of €50.
In the beginning of 2016 I helped compose a poem and designed a postage stamp which was shown at an AHEAD conference in DCU. Both are soon to be shown in the Education Room in the National Gallery of Ireland.
